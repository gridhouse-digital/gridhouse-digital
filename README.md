## About Gridhouse

Gridhouse Digital is an **AI-first digital engineering studio** building practical software, automation, and intelligent operational systems.

We focus on products that help healthcare organizations, service businesses, and growing teams manage work more efficiently—from compliance training and employee operations to AI assistants, workflow automation, and custom SaaS platforms.

Our work combines thoughtful product design, dependable engineering, and human-controlled AI.

## What We Build

- **AI operations assistants** with tool restrictions, approval gates, monitoring, and human oversight
- **Healthcare operations software** for hiring, onboarding, training, compliance, and workforce management
- **WordPress and LearnDash platforms** with custom plugins, dashboards, integrations, and reporting
- **Modern websites and web applications** built around measurable business outcomes
- **Workflow automation and system integrations** connecting fragmented business tools

## Current Focus

- 🏥 Building the **Healthcare Operations Management System — HOMS**
- 🤖 Developing managed **AI operations assistants** for professional-service teams
- 🎓 Expanding compliance-training infrastructure for healthcare organizations
- 🧩 Creating production-ready WordPress plugins for employee and administrative dashboards
- 🚀 Turning internal tools and prototypes into focused SaaS products
